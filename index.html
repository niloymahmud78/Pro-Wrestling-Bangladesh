Welcome To Pro Wrestling Bangladesh (PWB)

It's owner "Niloy Mahmud" always had a dream to be a wrestler, And be the first ever person to start a wrestling promotion in bangladesh. He is training about 20 persons to be a wrestler.He have a very uniqe knowledge that he invented the way to be played wrestling without getting injured. He made the training way so much easy that a 10 year kid can wrestle too.He started his first jurney almost 13 years ago,in 2008. In 2010 he became very serious about wrestling that he started to teach others how to wrestle.He started with his cousins and his younger brother. After that many of other peoples got interested and sarted learning from him.At the age of 15, he got obssed with wrestling and set his goal to build a wrestling promotion.
